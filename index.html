About Skillionair IBM Maximo Training

Skillionair is a leading professional training initiative focused on IBM Maximo – the world’s leading Enterprise Asset Management (EAM) solution.

We empower students, IT professionals, and working executives to become job-ready in both Functional and Technical aspects of Maximo.

Industry-Oriented Training
Learn Asset & Location, Work Orders, PM, Inventory, Purchasing.
Technical Expertise
Maximo Architecture, DB, Automation Scripts, Integration, Reporting.
Real-Time Projects
Simulated projects to understand workflows and problem-solving.
Job Assistance & Interview Prep
Practical knowledge, scenario-based questions, resume guidance.
